The Position

As the AI Solutions Architect, you will be an integral part of FullStack’s AI Lab, driving both AI sales and strategic consulting initiatives. You will be responsible for business development, thought leadership, and content creation that positions the company as a leader in AI-driven solutions. This role requires a strong blend of technical knowledge, sales acumen, and a visionary approach to artificial intelligence. You will work closely with clients to design AI strategies that align with their business objectives and lead a cross-functional team to deliver transformative AI projects.

Strategic AI Consulting & Technical Expertise

Advise clients on the implementation and integration of AI technologies to address business challenges and foster innovation.

Lead the development of AI transformation roadmaps and strategies, ensuring alignment with clients’ long-term goals.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact AI projects, ensuring timely delivery and measurable outcomes.

Thought Leadership & Influence

Serve as a recognized thought leader in AI, contributing to industry forums, conferences, and events.

Publish blogs, whitepapers, and articles on AI trends, applications, and success stories, establishing yourself and the consultancy as trusted experts in the field.

Help establish best practices for the FullStack AI Lab, guiding a team of data scientists, AI engineers, and business consultants.

Develop and refine AI solution offerings, ensuring they stay relevant and cutting-edge in a fast-evolving market.

Collaborate with marketing teams to craft go-to-market strategies for AI products and services.

Business Development & Sales

Drive revenue growth by identifying and closing AI-driven business opportunities with new and existing clients.

Collaborate with sales teams to develop AI proposals, pricing strategies, and sales pitches tailored to client needs.

Build and nurture relationships with key stakeholders to promote AI services and expand the consultancy’s market presence.
